Festival Youth Orchestra

Print Page | SHARE PAGE

Festival Youth Orchestra (FYO) is a three-week full symphony orchestra program open to intermediate to advanced-level strings, brass, winds, and percussion between the age of 13-18.

Tonight, Ben Bolter, former FYO faculty, will be taking the podium leading Festival Youth Orchestra in their 14th Season. With a background as a player in NEC’s Preparatory School and College orchestras, Ben first appeared as a conductor with the National Symphony Orchestra, and is currently Director of Orchestras at Chicago's Merit School of Music.


The Program (subject to change)
Mozart: Overture from Marriage of Figaro
Sibelius: Finlandia
Beethoven: Symphony No. 3 'Eroica'
Copland: Hoedown from Rodeo

Date: July 26, 2013 - 7:30:PM
Price: Free
Location: NEC’s Jordan Hall
